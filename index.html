The Story

Mr. Ashok Kumar started his 50- acre kinnow plantations in 2010 with the aim of producing 100% organic citric fruits in his home town in Haryana. In the last 5 years, the farm’s produce has made a name for itself in the market, known for its superior quality and taste. The products have been 100% organic since 2018 (pending government certification). Born in an agricultural family, Mr. Ashok firmly believes in being a steward of the land and in sustainable business practices. The plantations are irrigated using drip irrigation methods so as to optimise the use of water. Two ponds have been constructed to harvest rainwater and store water from the canal so as not to deplete the groundwater table. The farms also offer gainful employment and fair livelihoods to the people of Sundrehti.

Far from the hum bum of city life and surrounded by lush green agricultural fields, Jakhar Farms offer more than just quality fruits.
